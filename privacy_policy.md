Tamarin PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: November 2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
This App may collect:
- Currency preferences and recent conversions (stored locally)
- App usage statistics (anonymous)
- Crash reports (anonymous)

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION
Information is used to:
- Provide and improve the App's functionality
- Remember your preferences
- Fix bugs and improve performance

3. DATA STORAGE
- All currency preferences are stored locally on your device
- We do not collect or store personal information on external servers
- Exchange rate data is cached locally for offline access

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
The App may use:
- Currency rate APIs for real-time data
- Analytics services (anonymous data only)

5. DATA SHARING
We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share personal information with advertisers
- Track your financial transactions

6. LOCATION DATA
We request location permission once during initial setup to:
- Detect your country and suggest appropriate local currency
- Pre-select your timezone for the timezone helper feature

Location Usage Details:
- Requested only once during app setup (not continuously tracked)
- Used only to determine your country/timezone
- Never stored on our servers or shared with third parties

The app works fully without location permission if you prefer to set currencies manually.
